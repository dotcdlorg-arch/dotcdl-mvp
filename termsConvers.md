# Trucking Terms with Sample Conversations

Simple English conversation snippets between inspector and driver.
Each term has one back‑and‑forth exchange that uses the term in context.

---

## ELD / LOGGING / HOS TERMS

### ELD – Electronic Logging Device – 电子行车日志设备

Inspector: "Please show me your ELD so I can review your last 8 days of logs."  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, here is my electronic logging device screen with my log history."

### Paper Log – 纸质日志

Inspector: "Why are you using a paper log instead of your ELD today?"  
Driver: "The ELD failed this morning, so I switched to a paper log to stay compliant."

### Time Card – 打卡记录

Inspector: "Do you normally record your local work on a time card?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, for local runs my company keeps a time card instead of a full logbook."

### Graph Grid – 日志图表

Inspector: "On your old logs, did you fill out the graph grid correctly each day?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I drew my duty status on the graph grid for every 24‑hour period."

### Log Entry – 日志条目

Inspector: "When did you make the last log entry in your system?"  
Driver: "I created my last log entry when I went from off duty to driving at 7 a.m."

### Transfer Data – 传输数据

Inspector: "Can you transfer data from your ELD to my system now?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I will use the transfer data option on the ELD menu."

### Web Service – 网络服务传输

Inspector: "Is your ELD set up to send logs by web service?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I can send my records directly to enforcement using web service."

### Email Transfer – 邮件传输

Inspector: "If web service fails, can you do an email transfer of your logs?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I can send an email transfer to the official address from my ELD."

### Malfunction – 故障

Inspector: "You reported an ELD malfunction yesterday; is it fixed now?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, the malfunction was repaired and the unit is working correctly today."

### Backup Paper Logs – 备用纸质日志

Inspector: "Show me your backup paper logs in case the ELD stops working again."  
Driver: "Here they are, inspector; I keep several backup paper logs in my permit book."

### 8 Days of Paper Logs – 8天纸质日志记录

Inspector: "Regulations require at least 8 days of paper logs in the truck; do you have them?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I have more than 8 days of blank paper logs ready to use."

### ELD Instruction Manual – ELD操作手册

Inspector: "Where do you keep the ELD instruction manual in this cab?"  
Driver: "The ELD instruction manual is in this side pocket, inspector."

### HOS – Hours of Service – 工作时间规定

Inspector: "Are you familiar with the basic HOS, or Hours of Service, rules?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I follow the Hours of Service limits every day."

### 11‑Hour Driving Limit – 11小时驾驶上限

Inspector: "How many hours have you driven today under the 11‑hour driving limit?"  
Driver: "I have driven 6 hours so far, inspector, so I am under the 11‑hour driving limit."

### 14‑Hour Window – 14小时工作窗口

Inspector: "What time did your 14‑hour window start this morning?"  
Driver: "It started at 5 a.m., inspector, so my 14‑hour window ends at 7 p.m."

### 30‑Minute Break – 30分钟休息

Inspector: "When did you take your required 30‑minute break today?"  
Driver: "I took a 30‑minute break at noon, inspector, after about 6 hours of driving."

### 70‑Hour/8‑Day Cycle – 70小时/8天周期

Inspector: "How many hours are left on your 70‑hour/8‑day cycle?"  
Driver: "I have 10 hours left in my 70‑hour/8‑day cycle, inspector."

### On Duty – 值勤状态

Inspector: "At what time did you first go on duty this morning?"  
Driver: "I went on duty at 6 a.m., inspector, when I started my pre‑trip inspection."

### Off Duty – 休息状态

Inspector: "How many hours were you off duty last night?"  
Driver: "I was off duty for 10 full hours, inspector, before I started work today."

### Sleeper Berth – 卧铺状态

Inspector: "Did you spend part of your break in the sleeper berth?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I slept 8 hours in the sleeper berth last night."

### Driving Status – 驾驶状态

Inspector: "Your ELD shows you in driving status for this period; is that correct?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I was moving the truck the whole time, so driving status is correct."

### Remaining Hours – 剩余小时

Inspector: "How many remaining hours of drive time do you have today?"  
Driver: "My ELD shows I have 3 remaining hours of driving, inspector."

### Violation – 违规

Inspector: "Do you see this HOS violation on your log from two days ago?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I understand there is a violation and I will be more careful."

### Out of Service (OOS) – 停运

Inspector: "Because of this serious issue, I must place you out of service."  
Driver: "I understand, inspector; while I am out of service I will not move the truck."

## SPECIAL STATUS TERMS

### Personal Conveyance (PC) – 私用驾驶模式

Inspector: "Did you use Personal Conveyance to drive from the receiver to this truck stop?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I was empty and off duty, so I used Personal Conveyance."

### Yard Move (YM) – 场内移动模式

Inspector: "Why is this move marked as Yard Move on your log?"  
Driver: "Inspector, I used Yard Move because I was just repositioning the truck inside the yard."

### Unassigned Driving Time – 未分配驾驶时间

Inspector: "There is unassigned driving time on this truck; can you explain it?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I forgot to log in that morning, so the driving shows as unassigned driving time."

### Edit Log – 编辑日志

Inspector: "Did you edit your log when you realized the status was wrong?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I used the edit log function and added a note about the mistake."

### Certify Logs – 认证日志

Inspector: "Have you certified your logs for the last seven days?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I certify logs at the end of each day as true and correct."

### Pre‑trip Inspection – 车前检查

Inspector: "When did you complete your pre‑trip inspection today?"  
Driver: "I did my pre‑trip inspection at 5:45 a.m., inspector, before I started driving."

### Post‑trip Inspection – 车后检查

Inspector: "Did you perform a post‑trip inspection when you parked last night?"  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I did a full post‑trip inspection and filled out my DVIR."

## WEIGH STATION VOCABULARY

Inspector: "We will also check each axle weight on the scale."  
Driver: "Yes, inspector, I want to make sure every axle weight is legal."

### Tandem axle – 双轴

Inspector: "Is this a tandem axle group on the trailer?"

### Drive axle – 驱动轴
